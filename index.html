Whitesky Jet

Trans Aviasi Sinergi (TAS) is also linked to Whitesky Aviation Group, an Indonesian giant company known for its’ reputation in providing rotary wings and private flight services.

Since HSI is a subsidiary company of Whitesky Aviation Group in managing all business activities in Airport, TAS is mandated to do flight support & ground handling services for Whitesky Jet, a subsidiary company of Whitesky Aviation Group.

TAS manage the handling of Hawker 800XP operated by Whitesky Jet in Halim Perdanakusuma Airport.
